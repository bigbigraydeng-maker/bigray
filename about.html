About Ray Deng 关于 Ray Deng

Entrepreneur based in New Zealand, working on AI-driven ventures across migration, property, and cross-border business. 常驻新西兰的创业者，从事移民、房产和跨境商业领域的 AI 驱动项目。

Building practical solutions that connect markets and create value across China, New Zealand, and Australia. 构建连接市场、在中国、新西兰和澳大利亚创造价值的实用解决方案。

Background 背景

What I Do 我的工作

I work on building AI-driven ventures that address real market needs across migration, property technology, and cross-border business. My focus is on creating practical solutions that connect Chinese and Australasian markets. 我致力于构建满足移民、房产技术和跨境商业领域真实市场需求的 AI 驱动项目。我的重点是创建连接中国与澳新市场的实用解决方案。

Experience 经验

Over the years, I've built and operated several ventures across China, New Zealand, and Australia. This includes platforms for workforce mobility, property investment technology, and cross-border business automation. 多年来，我在中国、新西兰和澳大利亚建立并运营了多个项目。这包括劳动力流动平台、房产投资技术和跨境商业自动化。

  • Cross-border business development between China and Australasia 中国与澳新之间的跨境商业发展
  • Property technology and investment platforms 房产技术与投资平台
  • Workforce mobility and migration solutions 劳动力流动与移民解决方案

Ray Deng, also known as Big Ray, has been building businesses across the China-New Zealand-Australia corridor for over two decades. Based in Auckland, New Zealand, Ray combines deep local market knowledge with global perspectives to create innovative solutions in AI systems, labor mobility, and cross-border commerce.

Ray Deng，也被称为大瑞，二十多年来一直在中国-新西兰-澳大利亚走廊建立业务。Ray 位于新西兰奥克兰，结合深厚的本地市场知识和全球视野，在 AI 系统、劳动力流动 和 跨境商业 领域创造创新解决方案。

As a serial entrepreneur and strategic operator, Ray focuses on leveraging AI and automation to solve complex business challenges. His ventures span workforce migration platforms, property technology, and intelligent business systems that connect markets and create opportunities across borders.
